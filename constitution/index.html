This is a living, breathing constitution that will change and evolve over the lifetime of this league. Changes will happen between seasons as often as possible and will be effective from that point moving forward. If you have suggestions for changes, please submit them to the league commisioner and it may be put to a vote for next season. This constitution will not cover every situation as there are too many variables to cover.

[1.0 League Entry Fee]

[1.1 League Engry Fee - $100 per team]

League Fee must be paid by draft day. Any player that has not paid by draft day may forfeit the right to draft depending on the circumstances, and will forfeit any weekly winnings back to the total prize pool.

[1.2 Trophy Fee - $8 per team]

There will be a one time trophy fee in the 2019 season to help recoup the cost of the trophy and engravings. The Trophy Fee will be 8$ per team and should be paid with the League Dues.

[2.0 Prize Payout Structure]

Payout Structure is based on the assumption of a 12 team league.

[2.1 Weekly Payout]

The highest scoring team each week will win $30, except during playoffs, totaling $360 for the season. (Weeks 1 - 12).

[2.2 Champion payout structure]

  • First - $600
  • Second - $200
  • Third - $40

[3.0 League Setup, Rules, and Rosters]

[3.1 League Setup]

The Money League will consist of 12 different teams all in the same conference. The schedule will be randomly generated at the start of each season. The Regular Season will consist of 1 Week Head to Head matchups for 12 weeks. There are no tiebreakers in regular season matchups. The 4 top seeded teams will compete in the playoffs weeks 13-16. Seeding is based on Win Percentage and then Total Points Scored as the tiebreaker. In the event of a playoff matchup tie, the higher seed will advance to the next round.

  • Playoffs Round 1: 2 (Week 13-14) Seeds (No.1 vs No.4)/(No.2 vs No.3)
  • Playoffs Round 2: 2 (Week 15-16)

[3.2 Draft]

Draft is Auction format with a $200 Auction budget. Draft will take place 1-2 weeks before season start on ESPN.

[3.3 Keepers]

  • One keeper per team per year. You can keep a player for Two years max, not including the year drafted (3 years total)
  • Keeper cost is the same as the draft cost last year + 2.5% of the total budget
  • A player must be kept for a minimum of 10% of your total budget. This would apply to all undrafted waiver wire pickups.

[3.4 Rosters]

  • Roster Size: 15
  • Starters: 9 (1QB / 2RB / 2WR / 1TE / 1FLEX / 1DST / 1K)
  • Bench: 6
  • Max # of each position: (4QB / 8RB / 8WR / 3TE / 3DST / 3K)

[3.4 Free Agency]

Free Agency will be Auction based. Each team will be granted a Player Aquisition Budget of $200 at the start of the season. There are no transaction limits and no minimum bids. Free Agent Auction Tiebreaker is based on the inverse of standings (reset each week). Waiver Period is 1 day.

[3.5 Trading]

Trades are highly encouraged in The Money League. The trade deadline is the Friday before Week 10 (Novemeber 8, 2019). Trades will never be vetoed except when collusion is apparent or league competition is severly stifled due to the trade. The League commisioner reserves the right to veto any trade they deem inconsistent with league competition and fair-play standards. This veto will only be invoked in extreme cases.

[3.5.1 Collusion]

If collusion occurs and can be proven then the players colluding will forfeit the chance to win money and will be kicked out of the league. In the event of collusion the pot will be given to the next placed players. Collusion includes any agreement, outside of the trade in question, that affects the trade in question: "Trade back" agreements, rentals, money changing hands, buying the other owner a beer, doing work for the other owner, etc.
